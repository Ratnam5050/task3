WONDERLA 


*Wonderla Amusement Park in Hyderabad, India began construction in 2016 and opened on January 20, 2018:
2013
*Wonderla Holidays Ltd acquired 49.57 acres of land in Ranga Reddy District, Andhra Pradesh for the park
2015
*Wonderla Holidays Ltd signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Andhra Pradesh government to build the park over the next four to five years
2016
*Construction began on the park
2018
*The park opened with the launch of its most notable ride, Recoil, a reverse looping roller coaster

Wonderla Hyderabad is part of the Wonderla chain of amusement parks, which is owned and operated by Wonderla Holidays Limited. The park is located in Raviryal, 28 kilometers from Hyderabad City, and features 28 land-based rides and 18 water-based attractions. Some say the park is kid-friendly and has fun water rides.
